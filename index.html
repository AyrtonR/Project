In 2020 a group of three friends struggled with finding high quality kebab meals in Oulu, Finland.

They made a decision that would change their lives, and the North Ostrobothnian kebab scene forever.

With their dream of a restaurant serving delicious kebab meals in a relaxed environment, they worked hard for months.

In February 2021, the newcomer of the Kebab business; SRS Kebab, opened its doors at the beautiful Myllykatu.

Ever since, the restaurant and its passionate staff have given lots of nice and tasty kebab moments to their customers.

The cornerstone of SRS Kebab is high quality Finnish kebab.

Cooked with love and talent, served with the best fresh ingredients.

Our menu is based on our own studies in the kebab field.

We have taken inspiration from places we look up to, and added our own spice.

Our cozy restaurant has space for 50 customers is open every day from 10 am to 4 am,
