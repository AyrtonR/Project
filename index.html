Back in 2020, a group of three friends struggled with finding high quality kebab meals in Oulu, Finland.

They made a decision that would change their lives, and the North Ostrobothnian kebab scene forever.

With their dream of a restaurant serving delicious kebab meals in a nice environment, they worked hard for couple of months.

In February 2021, the newcomer of the Kebab business, SRS Kebab, opened its doors at the beautiful Myllykatu.

Ever since the restaurant and its passionate staff have given a lots of tasty and nice kebab moments for their customers.

The cornerstone of SRS Kebab is high quality Finnish kebab.

Cooked with love and talent, served with the best fresh ingredients.

Our menu is based on our own study in the kebab field.

We have borrowed something from the ones that we look up to, and added some of our own spice.

Our cozy restaurant with space for 87 customers is open every day from 9am to 5am,
